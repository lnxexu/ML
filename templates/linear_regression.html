Welcome to FitPredictor

FitPredictor is an innovative web application designed to help users forecast their weight based on their lifestyle choices, including exercise frequency, dietary habits, and environmental factors. By leveraging advanced predictive analytics, FitPredictor allows users to upload their data in CSV format or enter it directly as JSON. The application utilizes a sophisticated linear regression model to analyze the input data and generate personalized weight predictions for the next six months.
